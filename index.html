FAQ

To help you make the right decision,
here are all the details you need:

What is the fee of a Spartan Gyros® franchise?

$25,000.

How much do I need to pay in royalties?

3% of your monthly gross sales.

How much do I need to invest to open up my own Spartan Gyros® store?

Total investment is an estimate of between US$ 140,000 and US$ 500,000.

How much do I need to pay in marketing?

2% of monthly gross revenue

How long does the franchise contract last?

5 Years

How much do I need to pay for training?

$5000per person.

What happens when the franchise contract expires?

You can renew for another 5 years.

What kind of support will I receive as a SPARTAN
GYROS® franchisee?

Site Selection

We will recommend different locations based in our demographics studies

Build-up

We will also provide guidance and support on appropriate build-out and décor for your location.

Training

You will spend 7 days at our corporate headquarters and Spartan Gyros® stores with our team of experts to ensure that you will receive the latest information related to operate your Spartan Gyros® store.

Opening Assistance

For the opening of the Restaurant, a trained representative will provide on-site pre-opening and opening training, supervision, and assistance to you for up to two weeks around your franchise’s opening.

Operations Manual

You will receive our detailed Operations Manual, which contains our established standards and procedures. This manual will be your guide for everyday operations and it will serve as a solid foundation to follow.

Marketing Assistance

We will provide assistance with the development of initial and ongoing marketing campaigns. We will help you with some of the best strategies to make sure that everyone in your community knows the Spartan Gyros® name!

Ongoing Support

As a Spartan Gyros® franchisee, you will be part of our family, and you will receive ongoing support as on-site visits to boost your business, regular consultation in operations and marketing, and continuous guidance to run your business.

How much money can I make?

The Federal Trade Commission regulates what Spartan Gyros® is allowed to disclose to potential franchisees.

As you follow the process of purchasing a Spartan Gyros® business, you will receive the Franchise Disclosure Document (FDD) that details more about the business opportunity and your expected startup expenses.

Do you offer financing?

Spartan Gyros® does not offer any direct financing, but we can recommend third party financing options for you.

What kind of space should I need to open a Spartan Gyros® store?

You need a commercial space in a high traffic location from 900sq ft to 1400 sq ft able to seat from 10 to 12 costumers.

Approximately how long is the process from the moment I sign until the moment I open my franchise?

This process can take over 4 months. It is important to highlight that there are many procedures to follow after signing the Franchise Agreement, like signing a lease, getting blueprints approval in the city where you will open your store, construction, certificates and other aspects that do not depend on the brand.

Do I have to be a chef, or learn how to cook?

To become a SPARTAN GYROS SPECIALIST, must have at least 6 months of related industry experience.

Spartan Gyros® has partnered with major food
distributors to ensure that its ingredients reach every store effortlessly. These products, designed for easy frying or baking following provided instructions, maintain Spartan Gyros®'s commitment to delivering high-quality items to customers. This streamlined distribution process not only simplifies operations for store owners but also guarantees consistent product quality across all outlets.

Will you train the store’s employees?

Initially, you must have two full-time people working the business, one of which must be you. One must be responsible for business operations and management; the other must always be a Certified Spartan Gyros Specialist (SPARTAN GYROS SPECIALIST). Both of you must successfully complete our initial training program. We will decide whether you and your SPARTAN GYROS SPECIALIST successfully complete the initial training program based upon knowledge test results and our observations of your ability to use the knowledge effectively.

How can you guarantee top quality products?

At Spartan Gyros, quality is our priority:

Premium Sourcing: We select the finest local ingredients for authentic Greek flavors.

Expert Chefs: Our skilled chefs ensure excellence in every dish, emphasizing freshness and taste.

Stringent Control: We maintain strict quality control measures at every stage of preparation.

Freshness Commitment: Our menu highlights the vibrant taste that comes from using only the freshest ingredients.

Hygiene Focus: A clean and hygienic kitchen environment is maintained to ensure food safety.

Customer Feedback: Your feedback guides our continuous improvement, ensuring we meet or exceed expectations.

Spartan Gyros guarantees a top-tier dining experience, where quality and authenticity meet in every bite.

How often are products sent to each store?

We ensure a seamless supply chain at Spartan Gyros® by providing you with detailed lists of products to be purchased on a weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly basis.

Our procurement process involves partnering with major distribution companies to secure the highest quality ingredients for our authentic Greek dishes. This proactive approach ensures a consistent and reliable source of premium Spartan Gyros®.

At least how many employees are necessary to operate a franchise?

The staffing requirements for a Spartan Gyros® franchise depend on factors like restaurant size and customer volume. Typically, roles include chefs, kitchen staff, front-of-house staff, managerial staff, and cleaning staff. Staffing needs are tailored to the specific operations and size of each franchise location.
